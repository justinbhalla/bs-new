Overview & principles

  • Explain first: Visuals are annotated with units, basis, and time span.
  • Source-linked: Each widget references the underlying dataset where feasible.
  • Deterministic: Calculations are simple, documented, and reproducible.
  • Graceful degradation: If a source stalls, we show last good value with a staleness badge.

Modules (what you’ll see)

Indices
Live NIFTY/Sensex snapshots with breadth (adv/decl/unch), sector deltas, and intraday range.
Calc: %Δ = (Last − PrevClose) / PrevClose.
Sector Heatmap
Market-cap weighted move by NSE industry; hover for top movers & weights.
Calc: Sector return = Σ(weightᵢ × returnᵢ).
Commodities
Spot/futures for Brent, Gold, Silver, Copper; INR where relevant with FX baseline.
Calc: INR series = USD × USDINR last.
Calendars & Trackers
Earnings, IPOs, policy events; tagged by sector, size, and impact where available.
SLA: refresh daily; event times in IST unless noted.

Design conventions

  • Green ↑ for positive moves; red ↓ for negative.
  • Grey hash = stale/partial data.
  • Tooltips include timestamp, unit, and source.

Key definitions

1D / 5D / 1M
Close-to-close change over the stated window; weekends/holidays skipped.
YTD
From last trading day of previous calendar year to last close.
Real-time
Exchange permitted latency; typically 1–15 minutes unless noted “live”.

Refresh & latency

  • Prices: Intraday every 1–5 minutes; stamps show last update.
  • Aggregates: Nightly recompute; sector weights & totals rebased daily.
  • Calendars: Daily 06:00–08:00 IST; ad-hoc during earnings weeks.

Data quality & fallbacks

  • Primary → Secondary → Cached last-good.
  • Outliers flagged via z-score; winsorized view on hover.
  • Manual overrides logged; visible in changelog.

Architecture (high-level)

Ingest
Scheduled pull from exchanges/APIs and public filings; schema-validated CSV/JSON.
Transform
Normalize, join, enrich (FX, sectors), compute derived fields; audit trail retained.
Serve
Edge-cached JSON for widgets; rate-limited; stale-while-revalidate for resilience.

We publish methods and caveats in each widget’s tooltip or “info” panel.

FAQ

Why doesn’t my number match another site?
Different timestamp, corporate actions, or currency basis. Hover the info icon for our basis and last refresh.
Can I export the data?
We’re adding per-widget CSV soon. For syndication or commercial use, see Licensing.
