Method
Constant SIP uses annuity math with per-period rate \(i=r/m\), periods \(n=mY\). FV = lump\(\cdot(1+i)^n\) + SIP\(\cdot\frac{(1+i)^n-1}{i}\cdot(1+i)^k\); \(k=1\) for start, \(0\) for end. Step-up SIP applies an annual increase \(g\) once every 12 contributions; Goal mode solves via bisection.
