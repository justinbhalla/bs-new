Method

Constant SIP uses the annuity formula with per-period rate \(i=\frac{r}{m}\), periods \(n=m\times Y\). Final Value (FV) = lump-sum\(\cdot(1+i)^n\) + SIP\(\cdot\frac{(1+i)^n-1}{i}\cdot(1+i)^{k}\), where \(k=1\) for contributions at start (annuity due) and \(k=0\) for end (ordinary). Step-up SIP applies an annual increase \(g\) once every 12 contributions and is solved via bisection for Goal mode.

Tips
  • “Start of period” assumes each SIP compounds for one extra period vs “End of period”.
  • Inflation only affects the display (real values), not the computation path.
  • Step-up applies annually (after each block of 12 contributions).
