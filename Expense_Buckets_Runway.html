Expense Runway

Estimate how many months your cash lasts. Enter your monthly income, monthly expenses, and liquid assets. Add a quick shock if needed.

Inputs

Runway

Rule of thumb

Target at least 3–6 months of runway. Under stress, cut discretionary spend first and rebuild the cushion once income stabilizes.
