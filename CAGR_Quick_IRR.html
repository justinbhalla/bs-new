Method

CAGR = \((\text{End} / \text{Start})^{1/\text{Years}} - 1\). IRR is solved via bracketed bisection on NPV, bounded \([-0.9999, 10]\) per period, tolerance \(1e{-7}\). Annualized using frequency \(m\): \((1+r_{period})^{m}-1\).
