Old vs New Tax Slab

Compare take-home across regimes for typical income bands.

TaxCompare
Browse Tools

Inputs

Salaried assumptions include a standard deduction by default. You can toggle common deductions for the Old regime.

Note: Typically capped to 10% of salary (14% for Central Govt.). Enter the allowed amount.
Default ₹50,000 for salaried in both regimes.
Custom slabs (optional)

If “Financial year = Custom”, these will be used. Add comma-separated bands (₹) and rates (%). Example bands: 0,250000,500000,1000000 with rates 0,5,20,30.

Results

We compute tax → add surcharge (if any) → add cess → apply Section 87A rebate where applicable. Marginal relief is not modeled.
