Allowed via paid license. Email licensing@batasutra.in with URL, territory, and duration.

Data (tables, feeds, downloads)

Permitted (no paid license)
  • Personal analysis and classroom use with attribution.

  • Bulk downloads, redistribution, or internal tools serving multiple users.
  • Commercial research, investor materials, or paid newsletters.

Key display & caching rules

  • Always show “Source: bataSutra” with a link to the originating page.
  • No removal of notes, caveats, or methodology; no re-hosting as your “original dataset”.
  • Respect cache windows; offline storage beyond cache requires explicit approval.

Prohibited uses

  • Reselling our raw files or APIs, or training models that replicate our products.
  • White-label redistribution without a sublicensing clause in your agreement.

Request a data license

  • Intended use & audience
  • Endpoints/fields needed
  • Refresh rate & cache window
  • Territory & term

Code & Open Source

Licensing policy

Our repositories are licensed per repo (e.g., MIT/Apache-2.0). See each repo’s LICENSE file. Using our code does not grant rights to our brand names, logos, or UI look-and-feel.

Attribution & notices

Keep copyright headers and third-party notices intact in source and any substantial binary distribution.

Contributions

By submitting a PR, you certify you wrote the code or have permission to contribute it under the repo’s license. We may request a CLA for large contributions.
