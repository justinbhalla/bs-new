Source catalog

Regulators & Ministries
  • RBI — monetary policy, banking stats, payments, FX reserves
  • SEBI — market circulars, disclosures, mutual fund data
  • MCA — corporate registry snapshots
  • DEA/MoF — budget documents, fiscal statistics
  • MoSPI — national accounts, CPI/WPI, IIP
Exchanges & Market Infrastructure
  • NSE/BSE — prices, volumes, corporate actions, indices
  • CCIL/FT — debt/derivatives reference data where available
  • MCX/NCDEX — commodity quotes & notices
Company Filings & Releases
  • Exchange filings — results, investor presentations, shareholding
  • Annual reports & sustainability disclosures
  • Earnings calls & transcripts (where licensed)
Government Portals & Surveys
  • Open Government Data (OGD) platform
  • Directorate releases (trade, GST, transport, power)
  • Sample surveys (NSS, PLFS) — as published
International & Benchmarks
  • IMF, World Bank, UN, BIS — macro & financial series
  • ICE/LME/NYMEX — where referenced for global context
Vetted Third-party Vendors
  • Licensed terminals/feeds for intraday or derived indicators
  • Credit bureau or alternative data — aggregated and anonymized

Quick badges

Official — government/regulator/exchange
Public — open to all with link
Licensed — paid or restricted use
Derived — our calculation from raw

Use & licensing notes

  • Official sources are used under their site terms; we link back and cite clearly.
  • Vendor data is shown in aggregate or as derived indicators per license terms.
  • Our derived series, charts, and code are licensed as stated on the story/tool page. See Licensing.

Attribution format

“Source: RBI; SEBI; NSE; Company filings; bataSutra calculations.”

Provenance & validation

  • Links: Stories and dashboards include a “Source/Methodology” box with URLs.
  • Versioning: When sources revise historical series, we re-run affected charts and note material changes.
  • Triangulation: High-impact claims are cross-checked with at least one independent series when feasible.

Methodology

See our general Methodology for charting rules, smoothing, and adjustments.

Update cadence

Bucket Typical frequency Lag Notes
Prices & indices Live / EOD Minutes to 1 day Exchange official close; index rebalances per schedule
Macro (CPI, IIP, GDP) Monthly / Quarterly ~2–8 weeks Revisions are common; we flag base-year changes
Company results Quarterly Hours to days From exchange filings; later annual report refinements possible
Budget & fiscal Monthly / Annual ~4–6 weeks Interim updates in budget session documents

Quality & caveats

  • Units & currency are labeled; we use INR unless stated.
  • Seasonal adjustment is noted; raw series are available where practical.
  • Outliers and breaks (method changes, base shifts) are annotated in charts.
