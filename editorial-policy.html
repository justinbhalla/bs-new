1) Mission & Scope

bataSutra publishes India-focused business, markets, and policy journalism. This policy applies to all editorial staff, contributors, editors, and designers.

2) Standards: Accuracy, Fairness, Context

  • Accuracy over speed: we verify facts before publication and correct swiftly when wrong.
  • Provide sufficient context and avoid misleading framing or clickbait headlines.
  • Seek diverse perspectives; reflect material counter-arguments where relevant.

3) Sourcing, Anonymous Sources & Right of Reply

  • Prefer named sources; anonymity requires editor approval and a clear public-interest rationale.
  • Corroborate claims with documents, data, or multiple sources when feasible.
  • Offer timely right of reply to subjects of critical coverage.

4) Data, Charts & Methodology

  • Publish methodology, caveats, and source links for data-led stories and graphics.
  • Charts must reflect underlying data faithfully; choices (axes, baselines, sampling) are justified.
  • We label modeled/estimated figures and avoid overstating precision.

5) Independence & Conflicts

  • Editorial and commercial functions are separated; sponsors do not influence coverage.
  • Disclose and manage actual or perceived conflicts; editors may reassign stories or require disclosures.
  • Editorial staff may be subject to trading blackouts and holdings rules where coverage overlaps.

6) Labelling: News, Analysis, Opinion, Sponsored

  • News: Reported facts with neutral tone.
  • Analysis/Explainer: Context and interpretation grounded in data and reporting.
  • Opinion: Clearly marked commentary.
  • Sponsored/Partner: Prominently labeled; editorial retains control over standards disclosures.

7) Corrections & Updates

  • Material errors: visible correction note detailing what changed and when.
  • Non-material clarifications and updates are noted in-line or in footers where appropriate.
  • Readers can request corrections at corrections@batasutra.in.

8) Embargoes & Off-the-Record

  • We honor embargoes we agree to; we do not accept conditions that compromise independence.
  • “Off the record” terms must be explicit and agreed beforehand.

9) Social Media & Personal Publishing

  • Staff should avoid partisan endorsements that undermine perceived neutrality.
  • Breaking news and corrections should route through editorial channels before posting.

10) AI & Automated Tools

  • AI may assist with research/production but never replaces editorial judgment; sensitive claims require human verification.
  • If AI meaningfully contributes to text or imagery, we disclose.

11) Privacy, Safety & Minors

  • Exercise heightened care with minors and vulnerable subjects; minimize harm and avoid gratuitous details.
  • Respect reasonable expectations of privacy unless overriding public interest exists.

12) Plagiarism & Attribution

  • We do not plagiarize. Quotations and unique reporting are attributed; images and data carry source credits.
  • Use of external material complies with license terms and fair dealing/fair use.

13) Feedback & Complaints

Write to editorial@batasutra.in or corrections@batasutra.in. We review and respond in good faith.

14) Changes

Material updates to this policy will be signposted with a new “Last updated” date above.

15) Contact

Questions? Contact editorial@batasutra.in.
